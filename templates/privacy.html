Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data Spendly collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only what is necessary to provide the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic logs such as page visits, used solely for keeping the Service running.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive personal data beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Provide and maintain your personal expense tracking dashboard.
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • Send essential service emails (e.g. password resets) — no marketing emails.
  • Diagnose bugs and improve the reliability of the Service.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect it against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may rely on third-party infrastructure providers (such as cloud hosting) to operate the Service. These providers have access to your data only to perform tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

We use Google Fonts to render typefaces on this site. Google may collect limited data as described in their own privacy policy.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking pixels.
